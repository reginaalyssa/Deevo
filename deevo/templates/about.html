About

Deevo is a Django-based web application originally developed as a final project submission for CS 50. The app allows you to read the Bible, write down reflections on your chosen verses each day, and help you easily keep track of and edit your written devotions.

Read

Read the Word of God and easily switch between 7 different versions of the Bible.

Reflect

Choose a verse that inspired you and write your thoughts, reflections, or insights about it.

Remain

Keep track of your devotions so you stay committed to your personal journey with your faith.
